Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the Service, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Service to you — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and supporting filters and reports. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating your account.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Your account password is stored using one-way hashing and is never readable by us. You may delete your account at any time, which permanently removes all associated data.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may use minimal third-party infrastructure (such as hosting providers) strictly to operate the Service. These providers are contractually bound to keep your data confidential and may not use it for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information available on our site. We will respond to all reasonable requests within a reasonable timeframe.
